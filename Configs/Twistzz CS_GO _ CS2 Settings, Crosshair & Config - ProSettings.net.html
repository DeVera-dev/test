Russel “Twistzz” Van Dulken is a professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player from Canada who is currently playing for FaZe Clan. He is the most decorated North American CS:GO player, having won the Antwerp Major in 2022 and the Intel Grand Slam in 2019, as well as winning multiple top tier tournaments and personal accolades.

He is the first Canadian player to win a CS Major tournament.
